About Face Yoga

Face yoga is a natural and holistic approach to skincare that involves a series of facial exercises and massages. These techniques help to tone and strengthen the muscles in the face and neck, improve circulation, and reduce stress and tension.

Benefits of Face Yoga

  • Reduces the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines
  • Lifts and tightens the skin
  • Improves skin tone and complexion
  • Relieves tension and stress in the face and neck
  • Promotes a more youthful and radiant appearance

Face Yoga Exercises

Exercise 1: The V

Place your index fingers on the inner corners of your eyebrows and your middle fingers on the outer corners. Gently lift the skin upwards and hold for 10 seconds. Release and repeat 5 times.

Exercise 2: The O

Make an "O" shape with your mouth and hold for 10 seconds. Release and repeat 5 times. This exercise helps to tone the muscles around the mouth and reduce the appearance of smile lines.
